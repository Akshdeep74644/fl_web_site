Attestation Services in Delhi

Document attestation or certificate attestation is the process of verifying the authenticity of a document or certificate by an authorised government body or agency. This process is commonly required when using a document in a foreign country. The purpose of attestation is to ensure that the document is genuine and that the signature, seal, or stamp on the document is authentic.

The attestation services in Delhi generally involves verifying the document at different authorities. It starts with the issuing authority, including the government department, educational institution, or notary public. Once the document is verified by the issuing authority, it gets attested by higher authorities, such as the Ministry of External Affairs or the Department of State. In some cases, the document may also require attestation from the embassy or consulate of the destination country.

The attestation services in Delhi involves the following steps:

> Notary Attestation: The document or certificate may need to be notarised by a notary public. The notary public verifies the identity of the document holder and ensures the document's authenticity.

> Home Department Attestation: Depending on the type of document, it may require attestation from the Home Department of the respective state where the document was issued. This step verifies the document's authenticity at the state level.

> Ministry of External Affairs (MEA) Attestation: After obtaining attestation from the Home Department, the document needs to be attested by the Ministry of External Affairs (MEA). The MEA is the central authority responsible for attesting documents for use abroad. The MEA verifies the document's authenticity and stamps or attaches a certificate to it.

> Embassy or Consulate Attestation:If the document is intended for use in a specific foreign country, it may require attestation from the respective embassy or consulate in Delhi. This step involves submitting the document to the embassy or consulate for verification and obtaining their stamp or seal.
